This work is part of the completion of my graduation project at the Higher Institute of Computer Science in Tunis el Manar to obtain the national degree of fundamental in computer science.
The project consists in developing a generic system for creating and executing banking stress test scenarios. This report presents the different phases of its realization, of which I have mixed techniques of the field of data analysis - using logistic regression- with methods in the field of banking risk management (such as the Monte-Carlo simulation method).
